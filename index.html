The Artbox Project

Welcome to the Box. This is a place where you can admire some of Canada's most iconic paintings at your own pace. In this room, you will find these pieces divided into categories which will lead you to related artwork. The ArtBox Project's goal is to attempt to recreate the atmosphere of a gallery space — time, movement and presence — and design an experience addressing the mind as well as the senses.
